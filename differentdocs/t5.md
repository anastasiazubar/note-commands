## Test 4 – Activation Group Policy Assignment Validation

### Objective

Ensure privileged Membership Groups are not directly referenced in crpa Allow Policies, Deny Policies, or Principal Access Boundaries when a corresponding Activation Group exists.

### Validation

1. Identify all privileged Membership Groups that have a corresponding Activation Group.

2. Retrieve all crpa Allow Policies, Deny Policies, and Principal Access Boundary Policies.

3. Review principals referenced within each policy.

4. Verify Membership Groups are not directly referenced in policy bindings when a corresponding Activation Group exists.

5. Verify the corresponding Activation Group is used instead.

6. Identify any policies that reference a Membership Group instead of its Activation Group.

### Failure Condition

A privileged Membership Group is referenced in an crpa Allow Policy, Deny Policy, or Principal Access Boundary Policy while a corresponding Activation Group exists.

### Examples

**Fail**

Policy Principal:

```text
gcp_ca_masta@misto.com
```

Expected:

```text
gcp_ca_masta_tatatata@misto.com
```

**Pass**

Policy Principal:

```text
gcp_ca_masta_tatatata@misto.com
```

### Alert

Critical severity email notification sent to crpa Operations team including:

* Policy Name
* Policy Type
* Membership Group Name
* Expected Activation Group Name
* Resource Scope
* Detection Timestamp
