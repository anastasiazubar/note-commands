Test 2 – TEALAS Access Expiration Validation
Objective

Ensure temporary access granted through TEALAS is removed after the approved access period expires.

Validation
Identify all Activation Groups managed through TEALAS.
Retrieve active memberships for each Activation Group.
Review membership expiration timestamps.
Verify memberships are automatically removed once the approved access period has expired (24 hours).
Identify any users whose membership expiration timestamp has passed but who remain members of the Activation Group.
Failure Condition

One or more users remain members of an Activation Group after their approved access period has expired.

Alert

Email notification sent to IAM Operations and TEALAS Support teams including:

Group Name
User ID / Email
Membership Expiration Timestamp
Detection Timestamp
Duration Beyond Expiration
