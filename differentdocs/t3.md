## Test 3 – Privileged Group Binding Validation

### Objective

Ensure privileged IAM bindings are assigned only to Activation Groups and not directly to Membership Groups.

### Validation

1. Identify all Activation Groups managed through TEALAS.

2. Locate the corresponding Membership Group for each Activation Group.

3. Retrieve IAM bindings associated with both groups.

4. Verify privileged IAM roles are assigned only to Activation Groups.

5. Verify Membership Groups do not contain privileged IAM role bindings.

6. Identify any privileged roles assigned directly to Membership Groups, bypassing the TEALAS activation process.

### Failure Condition

One or more privileged IAM roles are assigned directly to a Membership Group.

### Alert

Critical severity email notification sent to IAM Operations and TEALAS Support teams including:

* Membership Group Name
* Privileged Role(s)
* Resource Scope
* Detection Timestamp
* Associated Activation Group
